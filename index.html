About Me

I am currently a student athlete studying Electrical Engineering at the University of Victoria. I'm in my fourth year at Uvic and have been a forward on the Vikes hockey team since I started my studies. I'm originally from Camrose Alberta and have lived in other parts of the province while playing junior hockey prior to attending university. In conjunction to my engineering degree I'm also pursuing a business minor. I see the business minor as a good compliment to my studies and will help me develop some valuable skills to be a better engineer. Uvic has been a great place for my educational and athletic goals to come together. For the past two hockey seasons I was given the opportunity to be a alternate captain and took on responsibilities to help lead my teammates.
